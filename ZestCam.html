Privacy Policy No Data Collected

Applies to: ZestCam (“the App”)
Effective Date: 2025-11-12

Summary

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require account creation, does not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs, and does not perform cross-site or cross-app tracking.

Data We Collect

None. During use, the app does not collect any of the following information:

  • Identity information (name, email, phone number, address, etc.)
  • Device or network identifiers (advertising ID, device ID, IP address, precise or coarse location, etc.)
  • Usage or diagnostic data (crash logs, performance metrics, screen views, session length, etc.)
  • Payment data or transaction records
  • Contacts, photos, calendar, microphone, camera, or other content

Permissions and Local Processing

If the app requests system permissions (such as camera, microphone, photos, or notifications), they are used solely to enable the related features. Any data accessed remains processed locally on your device; we do not transmit or store it on our servers.

If you deny permission, only the related feature will be unavailable. Other parts of the app remain fully functional.

Third-Party Sharing and Tracking

  • No sharing: We do not share any user data with third parties.
  • No tracking: We do not engage in cross-site or cross-app tracking.
  • No third-party SDKs: The app does not integrate analytics, advertising, or behavioral tracking SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us using the details below; we will handle it promptly in accordance with applicable laws.

Data Retention

Because we do not collect any personal data, there are no data retention, export, or deletion procedures applicable.

Security

The app follows a data-minimization principle: by not collecting data, privacy and security risks are inherently reduced. Please keep your device and the app updated to ensure the highest level of protection.

Changes to This Policy

If we introduce new features in the future that require data processing, we will update this policy and indicate the new effective date on this page.
